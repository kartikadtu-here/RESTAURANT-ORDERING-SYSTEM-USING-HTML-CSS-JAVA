Our Story

Orkar was born from a love for the rich and underrated flavours of Assam. Our founders wanted to bring authentic Assamese home cooking to the table — alongside beloved Indian dishes from across the country.

Every recipe at Orkar is rooted in tradition, cooked with locally sourced ingredients, and served with warmth. We believe food is the best way to celebrate culture.
